How it works

Three layers. One habit.

Layer 01 🤖
Gemini builds your plan

Enter your age, weight, height, and health goal. The Gemini agent generates a personalized weekly plan: minutes to walk, dietary habits to build, and a clinical protocol adapted to your condition. It adjusts every week based on how you did the week before.

Layer 02 🤝
Meet your buddy

Gemini matches you with one or two people nearby who share your goal and age range. Your buddy never sees your clinical data. They only see whether you completed your week. That quiet accountability is enough to change behavior.

Layer 03 🗺️
Explore your city

As you walk, zones on your neighborhood map light up. Each week Gemini generates a mission tied to a specific area. Complete it with your buddy and unlock a shared achievement. The walking becomes the game mechanic.

AI-native operations

Gemini runs the business. Not us.

Every decision between a user signing up and receiving their weekly report is made by the Gemini agent. No human operator touches any workflow.
